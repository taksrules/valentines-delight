## Recommended Image Specifications
- **Format**: JPG or PNG
- **Aspect Ratio**: Square (1:1) works best for the polaroid-style frames
- **Size**: 800x800px or larger
- **Style**: Romantic, candid moments that tell your story

## Alternative: Use URLs
You can also use external image URLs by editing `src/app/config.ts` and changing the `memoryPhotos` array URLs.
